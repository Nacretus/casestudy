About The Company

Bright Corporation

Bright Corporation is a pioneering technology company that is lighting the path to the future. Founded with the vision of creating a world where technology and humanity coexist in harmony, Bright Corporation specializes in developing innovative, futuristic technologies that aim to enhance and enrich human life.
Our flagship product, the “Talking Contact Lenses”, is a testament to our commitment to pushing the boundaries of what’s possible. These smart lenses are designed to assist individuals with visual impairments, providing them with a new way to interact with the world. Using advanced AI and miniaturized electronics, these lenses can recognize text and objects, and relay this information to the user through audio cues.
